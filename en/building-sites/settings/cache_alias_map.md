 **Name**: Enable Context Alias Map Cache  
**Type**: Yes/No  
**Default**: Yes  
**Available In**: Revolution 2.7.0+

When enabled, all Resource URIs are cached into the Context. Enable on smaller sites and disable on larger sites for better performance.
